Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 25, 2023

Welcome to Prompt Store. This Privacy Policy describes how ALL TIPS NOW ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide AI-powered prompt generation services, we collect the following:

  • Images: When you use our "Image to Prompt" feature, the images you select are uploaded to our server and processed via the Gemini AI API.
  • Device Identifier: We collect a unique Device ID to associate your generated prompts with your device and prevent abuse.
  • Network Information: We monitor network status (WiFi/Data) to ensure smooth image uploads and ad delivery.

2. Use of AI (Gemini API)

Our app uses the Google Gemini AI model to analyze your uploaded images and generate descriptive prompts. By using this feature, you acknowledge that the images are processed by Google's generative language services. We do not use your private images for training our own models.

3. Storage & Media Permissions

Based on your Android version, we request access to your storage:

  • Read/Write External Storage: Required to pick images from your gallery and save downloaded AI-generated images or prompts to your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob uses the Advertising ID to show relevant ads and comply with privacy regulations.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): To send push notifications about new features or prompt updates.
  • Google UMP (User Messaging Platform): We use this to collect GDPR-compliant consent from users in the EEA and UK.

5. Data Retention

Uploaded images and generated prompts are stored on our secure servers managed by ALL TIPS NOW. We implement security measures to protect your data, but please note that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Advertising Disclosure

This app displays advertisements via Google AdMob. These ads help us keep the service free. You can manage your ad preferences through your Android device settings (Google > Ads).

7. Children's Privacy

Prompt Store is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover a child has provided us with personal data, we will immediately delete it from our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
